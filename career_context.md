## Section 2: Project Deep-Dives (The "Updates")

### 1. Prism AI (Biometric Intelligence Platform)
* **Original Build (2025):** A production-grade SaaS combining Computer Vision (Face ID) and Generative AI (RAG). I built a distributed microservices architecture separating the "Manager" (Node.js/GraphQL) from the "Brain" (Python/FastAPI) to handle face recognition and document intelligence.
* **2026 Reflection & Update:** "I am refactoring this for **Edge-First Privacy**. I am currently migrating the liveness detection logic from the Python server to **Client-Side WebAssembly (WASM)**. This eliminates server 'cold starts,' reduces latency for users with poor connections, and ensures biometric data is processed locally on the device—enhancing trust and privacy in sensitive communities."

### 2. RefugeLink (AI-Powered WhatsApp Chatbot)
* **Original Build (2024):** A decision-tree chatbot using Twilio and basic NLP for refugee assistance.
* **2026 Reflection & Update:** "I am refactoring the NLP engine to use **Gemini 3 Flash**. Because Flash is incredibly fast and cost-effective, I am moving from simple keyword matching to full semantic understanding in local dialects (Runyankore/Luganda). This allows refugees to voice-record their needs rather than typing, bridging the literacy gap."

### 3. HostelEase (Student Housing Platform)
* **Original Build (2024):** A MERN stack app with manual filters for availability.
* **2026 Reflection & Update:** "I am upgrading the search experience from static filters to a **Conversational Agent**. Students can now tell the app: *'I need a quiet place near the Science Block for less than 400k,'* and the AI infers intent to rank listings, replacing the manual filtering process."

### 4. RentalTrack (Offline-First App)
* **Original Build (2025):** React Native offline-first management tool.
* **2026 Reflection & Update:** "I am adding an 'On-Device Legal Assistant.' This uses a lightweight, distilled model to help landlords and tenants draft fair, legally binding tenancy agreements automatically, ensuring legal protection without needing a lawyer."
